# Influencer Personal Brand Website

A premium personal brand website designed for modern creators, influencers, entrepreneurs, and wellness advocates.

The goal of this project is not simply to showcase products, but to establish trust, build authority, grow a community, and convert visitors into loyal followers and customers.

---

## Vision

People don't buy products.

People buy people.

This website positions the influencer as the brand while showcasing trusted products, valuable content, and opportunities to work together.

---

## Objectives

- Build a memorable personal brand
- Increase social media followers
- Showcase valuable content
- Promote recommended products
- Collect email subscribers
- Generate partnership opportunities
- Build community

## Philosophy

Build the person.

The products will follow.

Every section should answer one question:

"Why should someone trust this person enough to follow or buy from them?"

If the answer isn't obvious, redesign the section.

---

## Development Rules

- Keep components small.
- Reuse sections where possible.
- Avoid unnecessary abstractions.
- Prefer composition.
- Mobile-first.
- Accessibility first.
- Performance matters.
